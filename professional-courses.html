Greyscale Portrait Course: Mastering Monochromatic Realism

Embark on a journey into the world of greyscale portraiture, where every stroke tells a nuanced story. In this course, learn the art of capturing depth and emotion through shades of grey. Explore techniques for shading, contrast, and fine detailing to bring your portraits to life. Whether you're a beginner or aspiring artist, join us to hone your skills in the timeless art of greyscale portraiture and create compelling, lifelike images.

Acrylic Painting Course: Vibrant Expressions on Canvas

Unleash your creativity with our Acrylic Painting course, designed to explore the versatility of acrylics on canvas. From color mixing to texture creation, this course covers fundamental techniques and advanced methods. Dive into the world of contemporary art as you experiment with layering, blending, and impasto effects. Whether you're a novice or an experienced painter, join us to discover the bold and expressive possibilities of acrylics.

Oil Painting Course: Classical Mastery on Canvas

Immerse yourself in the rich, classical world of oil painting with our specialized course. Learn the time-honored techniques of layering, glazing, and impasto to create luminous and textured masterpieces. Explore the unique properties of oil paints, from blending subtle gradients to achieving bold, vibrant colors. Whether you're a seasoned artist or a beginner, join us to elevate your artistry through the centuries-old tradition of oil painting.

Watercolor Painting Course: Transparent Beauty on Paper

Experience the delicate beauty of watercolor painting in our specialized course. Dive into the unique properties of watercolors, mastering techniques such as wet-on-wet, dry brushing, and layering. Explore the interplay of light and transparency to create ethereal and luminous artworks. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced artist, join us to unlock the captivating world of watercolor and express yourself through its fluid and vibrant medium.

Digital Art & Illustration Course: Navigating the Virtual Canvas

Enter the dynamic realm of digital art and illustration in our comprehensive course. From mastering digital tools to creating captivating illustrations, this course is tailored for both beginners and experienced artists. Explore the limitless possibilities of digital mediums, including digital painting, vector graphics, and concept art. Whether you're interested in character design, digital landscapes, or graphic storytelling, join us to unleash your creativity in the ever-evolving world of digital art.

Fashion Designer Croquis Course: Sketching Style and Elegance

Step into the world of fashion illustration with our Fashion Designer Croquis course. Learn the art of sketching dynamic and proportionate fashion figures that serve as the foundation for your design concepts. From elongated silhouettes to expressive poses, this course covers essential techniques for bringing your fashion visions to life on paper. Whether you're an aspiring designer or simply passionate about fashion, join us to refine your croquis skills and infuse your sketches with style and elegance.

Visual Art Studio

One of the most creative art studio based out of Kharghar, Navi Mumbai, the Visual Art Studio, was established in the year 2019 by a passionate artist Mrs. Gayatri Gajanan Patil who were highly inspired by visual art. Their passion led them to develop an enthusiastic studio that was driven to provide competent and professional fine art services to students of all ages.
