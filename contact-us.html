Visual Art Studio

One of the most creative art studio based out of Kharghar, Navi Mumbai, the Visual Art Studio, was established in the year 2019 by a passionate artist Mrs. Gayatri Gajanan Patil who were highly inspired by visual art. Their passion led them to develop an enthusiastic studio that was driven to provide competent and professional fine art services to students of all ages.
