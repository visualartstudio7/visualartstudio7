Ganpati Clay Idol Making Workshop

Immerse yourself in the sacred and artistic world of Lord Ganesha with our Ganpati Idol Making Workshop. This hands-on experience is designed for enthusiasts of all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned artisans.

During this workshop, participants will delve into the traditional techniques and modern innovations involved in crafting eco-friendly Ganpati idols. From molding the clay to intricately shaping the features of the beloved deity, our skilled instructors will guide you through every step of the process.

Beyond the technical aspects, this workshop is a celebration of creativity, spirituality, and cultural expression. Participants will not only learn the craft of idol making but also gain insights into the symbolism and significance of Lord Ganesha in Hindu mythology.

Join us for an enriching and joyful experience where you can express your devotion through the art of creation. Whether you're looking to learn a new skill, connect with cultural traditions, or simply engage in a unique and spiritually uplifting activity, our Ganpati Idol Making Workshop is the perfect platform to unleash your creativity and celebrate the divine. Let your hands mold not just clay but also a heartfelt connection with the revered Lord Ganesha.

Visual Art Studio

One of the most creative art studio based out of Kharghar, Navi Mumbai, the Visual Art Studio, was established in the year 2019 by a passionate artist Mrs. Gayatri Gajanan Patil who were highly inspired by visual art. Their passion led them to develop an enthusiastic studio that was driven to provide competent and professional fine art services to students of all ages.
