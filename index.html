Our Latest Workshops

Ganpati Idol Making Workshop

Immerse yourself in the sacred and artistic world of Lord Ganesha with our Ganpati Idol Making Workshop. This hands-on experience is designed for enthusiasts of all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned artisans.

Diwali Lantern Making Workshop

Step into the enchanting world of light and color with our Diwali Lantern Making Workshop. This immersive experience invites participants of all ages to discover the joy of crafting personalized lanterns, adding a touch of artistic flair to the Festival of Lights.

Portrait Rangoli Workshop

Embark on a creative journey where vibrant hues come together to breathe life into intricate portraits with our Portrait Rangoli Workshop. This unique and captivating experience invites participants to explore the fusion of traditional rangoli artistry with the dynamic world of portraiture.
