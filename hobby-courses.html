Resin Art Course: Unleashing Creative Fluidity

Dive into the captivating world of resin art with our comprehensive course. Explore the unique properties of resin as a medium and learn to create mesmerizing, glossy masterpieces. From understanding the basics of resin mixing to incorporating pigments and additives, this course is designed for both beginners and enthusiasts. Discover the art of pouring, swirling, and manipulating resin to produce stunning abstract pieces. Join us and embark on a journey where creativity flows seamlessly.

Mandala Art Course: Harmony in Symmetry

Immerse yourself in the meditative and symmetrical world of mandala art. This course is a deep dive into the creation of intricate, balanced mandalas. Learn the symbolism behind different elements of mandalas and master the techniques of drawing and coloring. Explore the therapeutic benefits of mandala creation while enhancing your artistic skills. Join us to experience the tranquility and beauty that mandala art brings, and let your creativity flourish in the circular patterns of harmony.

Indian Folk Art Course: Tradition on Canvas

Discover the rich tapestry of Indian folk art through our engaging course. From Madhubani to Warli, explore the vibrant traditions and storytelling embedded in each art form. Learn the characteristic motifs, colors, and techniques unique to various regions of India. Immerse yourself in the cultural narratives behind these art forms and bring them to life on your canvas. Join us to celebrate the diversity and beauty of Indian folk art while honing your skills in this culturally rich journey.

Lippan Mirror Art Course: Crafted Elegance

Unveil the charm of Lippan Mirror Art through our specialized course. Dive into the traditional craft of mirror embellishments on mud surfaces, creating a stunning interplay of light and texture. Learn the intricate process of designing patterns, embedding mirrors, and finishing with finesse. This course is a fusion of traditional artistry and modern aesthetics, allowing you to create contemporary pieces rooted in cultural elegance. Join us and bring the sparkle of Lippan Mirror Art into your artistic repertoire.

Fabric Art Course: Painting with Textiles

Explore the limitless possibilities of fabric as a canvas in our Fabric Art course. From basic textile techniques to advanced fabric painting, this course offers a hands-on experience in creating unique and personalized fabric artworks. Dive into the world of color blending, shading, and texture manipulation on various fabric mediums. Whether you're a beginner or looking to refine your fabric art skills, join us to transform ordinary textiles into extraordinary pieces of visual expression.

Visual Art Studio

One of the most creative art studio based out of Kharghar, Navi Mumbai, the Visual Art Studio, was established in the year 2019 by a passionate artist Mrs. Gayatri Gajanan Patil who were highly inspired by visual art. Their passion led them to develop an enthusiastic studio that was driven to provide competent and professional fine art services to students of all ages.
