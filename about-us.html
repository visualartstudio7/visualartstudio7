About Visual Art Studio

Established in 2019, the Visual Art Studio, situated in Kharghar, Navi Mumbai, stands out as one of the most innovative art studios. Founded by the passionate artist Mrs. Gayatri Gajanan Patil, who draws inspiration from visual art, this studio is dedicated to providing proficient and professional fine art services to individuals of all age groups.

Similar to the various forms of visual art such as drawing, graphics, painting, sculpture, and decorative arts, the Visual Art Studio is committed to delivering comprehensive art education to its students. The studio aims to foster the development of artistic skills, encouraging students to explore and understand their innate creative abilities. Through this process, the institute directs their creativity towards constructive and meaningful objectives.
